Before you subscribe

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

Yes — Stripe requires a card to initiate the trial, but you won't be charged until day 15. You can cancel anytime before that with no cost.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your Stripe customer portal. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens after I subscribe?

You'll be redirected to fill in your subscriber profile — your field, subfields, and keywords. Your first digest will arrive on the next Monday after your profile is complete.

How do I cancel?

E-mail [email protected] or click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any weekly send. No questions asked.

Is my payment information secure?

All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the most trusted payment processors in the world. We never see or store your card details.
